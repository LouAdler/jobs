Director of Engineering — Platform, Developer Tools & Enterprise Growth

Remote (U.S. & Canada) | Full-Time | 1Password

Role Overview

At 1Password, we’re building the secure access foundation for the AI-powered, multi-device workplace. As Director of Engineering, you’ll lead one of the most strategically important divisions in our company — a cross-functional organization of over 30 engineers and managers responsible for the infrastructure that powers secure identity, access, and developer experiences across our enterprise platform.

This division owns high-leverage systems used by tens of thousands of teams globally, including APIs, SDKs, developer tooling, authentication frameworks, and core extensibility services. You’ll lead delivery, scale the organization, and influence company-wide product and technical strategy.

The Scope and Challenge

Build and Scale a High-Performance Engineering Organization: Grow and evolve a multi-team engineering division with 30+ people, ensuring clear team structure, strong management layers, and consistent delivery.

Deliver a Complex, Secure, and Extensible Platform at Scale: Lead the design and delivery of the systems that power enterprise and developer use cases—SSO, provisioning, APIs, SDKs—with reliability and extensibility.

Drive Strategic Technical Roadmaps That Shape the Business: Partner with executive leaders across product, design, and GTM to define and deliver platform strategy that supports adoption, integrations, and growth.

Employee Value Proposition (EVP)

Purpose: Lead the evolution of a secure platform that empowers businesses to grow without compromising trust or flexibility.

Growth: Operate with full ownership and influence in one of the company’s most critical growth domains, directly tied to product expansion and enterprise success.

Motivators: This is a rare opportunity to lead at scale, solve complex technical and organizational challenges, and collaborate with a high-trust, high-performance team.
